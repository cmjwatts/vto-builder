## How a client uses this

1. Hits **Copy entire V/TO** (or downloads `.md`).
2. Opens Ninety → **Vision tool**.
3. Pastes each block into the matching field — Core Values into Core Values, Niche into Niche, and so on. The section names match Ninety's field names.
4. Refines with their coach or Senior Leadership Team. The builder is the warm-up; the team agreement is the real work.
